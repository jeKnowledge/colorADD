Eight years was the time needed for Miguel Neiva to transform his masters thesis into an ethically responsible business: to create a code that allows color blind people to identify colors.

And so 2010, ColorADD was born - now an exemple to follow amongst ethically responsible businesses.

Our business model is based in a co-creation system with companies and municipalities.

Today, thanks to this system, there are several brands and entities using the ColorADD code to improve the lives of their users. In total, there are currently more than 300 partnerships worldwide.

SOCIAL

ColorADD.Social

ColorADD.Social is a non-profit association dedicated to
promote social integration of the colorblind people.

Born in 2012, it's main concern is to raise awareness to the colorblindness issue, especially near the school community, training and contributing to an Inclusive School, by promoting the early screening of colorblindness in childhood, and granting color accessibility in Libraries and School Classes through the adoption of the ColorADD code.
