WHAT IS

COLORBLINDNESS

What is it?

There are different causes of color blindness. For the vast majority of people with deficient color vision the condition is genetic and has been inherited from their mother. Most colorblind people are able to see things as clearly as other people, but they are unable to fully ‘see’ colors. There are different types of colorblindness and there are extremely rare cases where people are unable to see any color at all.

The effect...

Because most of today’s communication is based on colors, this invisible limitation makes color blinded people with feel excluded from society

Colorblindness impacts…

  • Approximately 5% of world population is colorblind, that means 350 million people;
  • 90% Of the worldwide communication is based on colors;
  • 17% Had their colorblindness undetected until the age of 20;
  • 73% Already felt ashamed for the chosen color;
  • 90% Ask for help when Buying clothes;
  • 41% Feels difficulties in Social integration
